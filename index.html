Juno Steele Gray

Writer. Artist. Adventurer.



Juno is originally from coastal Virginia and now resides in Alabama. They firmly believe they can learn to do just about anything, and so far they have tried their hand at writing, roller skating, dancing, painting, playing flute and piano, acting, Mandarin Chinese, rock climbing, sailing, sewing, and more. Their poetry has appeared or is forthcoming in Gone Lawn, Pine Hills Review, JAKE, and more. They have attended or will attend residencies with Chestnut Review and the Writer's Colony at Dairy Hollow and received scholarship from the McCormack Writing Center. They have never denied the wild horse in themself.
