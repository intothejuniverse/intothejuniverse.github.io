Juno Steele Gray

Writer. Artist. Adventurer.



Juno is originally from coastal Virginia and now resides in Alabama. Their poetry has appeared in Gone Lawn, Pine Hills Review, JAKE, and more. They have attended residencies with Chestnut Review and the Writer's Colony at Dairy Hollow and received scholarship from the McCormack Writing Center. They have never denied the wild horse in themself.
